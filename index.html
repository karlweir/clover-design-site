Clover Designs is focused on providing high quality service and customer satisfaction. We will do everything we can to meet your expectations whilst producing the piece of furniture designed for you.

From our workshop near Durham, Clover Designs manufacture bespoke furniture using medium density fibreboard (MDF) as it's core material.

Each item is spray painted to your choice of colour.

With a variety of products and designs to choose from, we're sure you'll be happy working with us. Look around our website and if you have any comments or questions, please feel free to contact us.

You can visit us at Tynemouth Market (Sundays), look at our products and have a chat about your bespoke project.
